Golden Triangle

India's golden triangle is a tourist circuit in India which connects the national capital New Delhi, Agra and Jaipur.

The Golden Triangle Tour covers Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur. The Golden Triangle Tour is the most popular tourist circuit in India. This incredible Golden Triangle Tour Itinerary comprises the three famous cities of India—Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur—each having grandeur, glory, and history.

Himalaya Tours

The Himalayan Tours will transport you to Yoga and Ayurveda retreats in India to heal your body, mind, and soul. Though the Himalayas' natural beauty draws in thousands of tourists a year, one of the main reasons so many people travel to the region is to take on the lofty peaks themselves. The Himalayas are home to the world's tallest mountain, and scaling it is a true feat for any skilled mountain climber.

South India Tours

South India is a mystic and magnificent destination that combines bright colours, fascinating cultures, and beautiful landscapes all largely undiscovered by tourists. Explore lush tea plantations or cruise the backwaters of Kerala.

The ideal time to visit tropical South India is during the winter season. The peak months of this season are from December to February when the weather is at its most pleasant.
